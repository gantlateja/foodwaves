A chafing dish is a metal cooking or serving pan on a stand with an alcohol
burner holding chafing fuel below it. It is used for cooking at table, notably
in Gueridon service, or as a food warmer for keeping dishes at a buffet warm.
Historically, a chafing dish (from the French chauffer,
"to make warm") is a kind of portable grate raised on a tripod,
